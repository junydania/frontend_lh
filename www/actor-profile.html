Akproro Videos

Bowoto Jephta popularly known as Akpororo is one of Nigeria’s finest comedians with swagger. He started comedy around 2008 and had his breakthrough in 2010 after his recognition at the AY Open Comedy Mic show. The lanky comedian cracked ribs at the 2013 edition of “The Experience’, an annual Christian gathering held at the Tafawa Balewa Square (TBS) in Lagos..

Akpororo in a typical manner akin to natives of Warri in Delta State, delivers his jokes looking mean and able to swing in between jokes depending on the audience. He really got a standing ovation at the gathering sharing godly jokes and displaying different and comical manners of praying among Christians.
